Roots of Turkish Rock

don't show

Roots of Turkish Rock is an event that explains how Turkish Rock Music was born. From the mid '60s and hippie era to '80s, the aim of the event is to explain how Rock and Roll came to Turkey and influenced Turkish singers and first songs written in Turkish.

There will be a 30-minute set after the conference, buy tickets for the event in which there are limited seats. July 25-26, 2022, (Monday, Tuesday). From 12:00 to 17:00, Set: 16:00 to 17:00, Congresium, Ankara.

SPEAKERS

Erkin Koray

Guitar player, singer, songwriter, composer

He's known as one of the inventors of Anatolian Rock and one of the first singers to make Psychedelic Rock and Hard Rock in Turkey.

Cahit Berkay

Guitar player, singer, songwriter, composer, founder of Mogollar

He's known with his work with Cem Karaca. He's also composed songs for Turkish movies such as "Selvi Boylum Al YazmalÄ±m."

Taner Ongur

Bass guitarist, singer, songwriter, composer, founder of Mogollar

He started his career listening to The Beatles and playing his first acoustic guitar. He worked with Cem Karaca and Mogollar.

Ahmet Guvenc

Bass guitarist, songwriter, composer, founder of Kurtalan Ekspres

He played with Baris Manco until his death. He composed the well known song of Baris Manco called "Gulpembe." He still plays in Kurtalan Ekspres.
